Pig Dice

Gameplay

The pig game is not that much complicated here are some tips to help you understand the game;
1) its a game between two players each has his/her turn to roll the dice.
2) whenever player1 rolls the dice and doesn't score anything the game tells player2 that it is his turn to roll the dice.
3) Each player get the chance to roll the dice x3 whatever he scores should be equal to total of 11.
4) whoever is first to 100 points wins the game.
5) the dice has 6 dots with 4 sides.
